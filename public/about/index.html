About me

Hi, I'm Tom.

I'm a software developer from Melbourne, Australia.

Before achieving a computer science degree and entering the software industry, I worked for several years in Japan teaching English as a foreign language.

I make software because I love having the capability to make beneficial and impactful tools for wide audiences using code, and that's something I aim for, hence the name of this blog.

Professionally, I'm interested in great user experiences and usability, frontend development, and functional programming.

Outside of my profession, I'm a sometime polyglot who loves fiction, interesting video games, and absurdist humor.

I'm an experienced public speaker with a background in teaching; if any of my personal projects pique your interest, and you're looking for a workshop or conference speaker then by all means get in touch :)
